Flooding

These are sites to tell about flooding within your local areas.

  • Red Cross

    • Information about the local American Red Cross Chapter

  • GIS Charleston

    • Allows a user to gather information about charleston areas from maps , document searches, road closures, flood maps, sea level rise, and more.

  • NOAA Maps

    • Great maps showing high tides, sea level rise, marsh migration. Slider that shows what areas affected by what water levels.

  • CCVATCH

    • Website has links to different resources regarding sea level rise, climate change, and flooding.
